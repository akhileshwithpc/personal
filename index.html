Dear Soulmate,

First of all, A very happy birthday to you. Look at you, you pretty girl! You turned 18. I know the journey has been crazy till now and I am happy that we are still a part of each other. This makes me realize how fast we are growing up and how far we have come. I feel grateful to be designated as your Best Friend.

Isn't it funny how it started and how we ended up together. It is all like as if we were destined to meet and progress together in the ups and downs of our life. I know there are more things that are yet to come and I am sure that we will face them fearlessly.

Despite being quite the opposite of one another, we still managed to get along a lot of times, It is understandable that our thoughts and perspectives might not match all the time, sometimes we will argue, sometimes we will fight, sometimes we will completely distance from each other, but speed-breakers on the roads are for our own safety, right? Everything can't be so fast and so smooth, Right? If we don't manage properly we might get into a devastating accident, Right? These small clashes are something that makes our bond grow more. “True love flourishes endlessly when nurtured with care; it never fades.”

Since we are in the initial phases of our careers, we might not be as consistent in communicating as we were some years ago, and I am sure that it won't affect us either. Because we know how valuable it is to progress in life. How important it is to not just survive but enjoy our lives to the fullest.

I had said it once, but it was in another context, today I will say it again but it will be in another: “I WISH MYSELF TO BE YOUR LIFETIME MUSIC”
